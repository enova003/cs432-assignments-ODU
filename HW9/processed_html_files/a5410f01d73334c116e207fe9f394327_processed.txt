ODUGlobal offers scholarships designed to aid our online military students and their family members.
Disabled Military Scholarship
In partnership with the Military Connection Center , we offer this annual $1,000 scholarship opportunity to current or former service members​ disabled in the line of duty, and their spouses and dependents.
Image
Disabled Military Scholarship Information
To qualify, you must be:
Current or former service members (all branches, including the Coast Guard, National & Air Guard or Reserves) with a documented disability sustained in the line of duty.
Spouses or dependents of a servicemember with a documented disability sustained in the line of duty.
Surviving spouses or dependents of personnel killed in the line of duty.
