All ODU students have access to online tools and software such as:
Microsoft Office 365 : Download or access online apps like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and more.
Zoom : Create your own web conference meetings for group work or study sessions.
Microsoft OneDrive : Cloud storage to create, edit, store and share files, up to 5TB total.
G Workspace for Education : Free use of Google's Workspace for Education apps, such as Google Drive.
