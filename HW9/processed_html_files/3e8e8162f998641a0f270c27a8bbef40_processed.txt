Research Development
The Research Development Office of the Division of Research and Economic Development (DivRED) supports ODU faculty researchers in a variety of ways, from grant search resources to project development, research team building, and editing for extramural proposals.
The Grant Development Specialists are available to provide the following services:
Assist with building collaborative teams
Analyze solicitations and guidelines
Strategize with faculty to strengthen proposals
Writing guidance including outlines, checklists and editing support
