Canvas
Canvas is ODU's learning management system. Your instructors may use Canvas to deliver course content, host discussion forums, and administer exams. You can access Canvas in a browser, and mobile apps are available.
Learn more about Canvas at ODU
Personal Learning Environment (PLE)
The PLE is an interface used in flexible online courses, designed by ODU's Center for Learning and Teaching. PLE has been integrated into Canvas.
Portable Media
The option to take courses by portable media is limited to students with specific academic or military experience and training who are enrolling in specific programs.
For portable media exam issues or technical assistance, contact the MEM Program Manager at mempm@odu.edu .
