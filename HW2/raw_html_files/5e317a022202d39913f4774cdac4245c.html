Overview

The Engineering Management and Systems Engineering (EMSE) Department at Old Dominion University is dedicated to excellence in teaching and research in critical areas related to the management of complex, technology-intensive organizations and systems. Our award-winning programs are directed at working professionals and traditional full-time students with technical undergraduate degrees.Â  The degrees are available on campus in a traditional classroom setting as well as online through synchronous web delivery.
